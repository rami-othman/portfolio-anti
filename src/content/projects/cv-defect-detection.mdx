## Model Training & Deployment

We faced significant challenges with varied lighting conditions on the factory floor. We utilized aggressive data augmentation, including synthetic shadow generation and contrast shifting, to make the model robust.

The final model weights, roughly 20MB after INT8 quantization, execute in ~12ms per frame, easily keeping up with the line speed.
